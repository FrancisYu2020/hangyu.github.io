Social Experiences

During my undergraduate period, I was not only devoted to my course work, but also participated in many volunteer activities to devote my time to help others in need. Among those volunteer activities, my favorite one was to voluntarily teach how to resolve Rubik's cube every Friday afternoon in an elementary school to enrich those students' after class activities and skills. Despite I was the teacher, I actually also learned things such as how to organize students and how to vary my teaching strategy from students to students. One more thing is that I am sharing what I like to them and the Rubik's cube community might be promoted for this small thing.

Other than volunteer experience, I also worked actively as the leader of the Magic Show and Magic Cube Club at Zhejiang University and that was where the Outstanding Student Leaders Awards came. I hosted FMC Asia 2017 Hangzhou regional sub-court which is a competition that requires to use the fewest move solution to resolve a Rubik's cube in one hour for each scramble. And I also hosted WCA Hangzhou Spring 2018 Open which had many different games and invited competitors from all over the world. I worked so actly in hosting these competitions because I love this hobby and hope to contribute my own effort to make a stronger community. The last experience is about table tennis. I also worked as a member in Table Tennis Club at Zhengjiang University and we also contribute our time to train students who registered table tennis class but had little experience in playing this sport. This program aims to help them pass the final exams of the table tennis class.
